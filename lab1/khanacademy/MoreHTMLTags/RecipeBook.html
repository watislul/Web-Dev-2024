Ingredients Quantity
Potato 5-7
butter around 2 or 3 spoon
salt 1 spoon of salt

Step 1: Cut your potatoes for a little pieces
Step 2: Put butter into a pan with a potato
Step 3: Salt your potato
Step 4: Wait around 15 minutes to frie potato
Step 5: After all this put your fried potato into plate
Step 6: Enjoy your meal!!!
